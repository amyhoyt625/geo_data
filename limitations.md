# Limitations

## Scope

This study is limited to two conferences, ICSA and ICSE. While these are
major venues in software engineering, they are not fully representative of
the field as a whole. Researchers who primarily publish in regional venues
may not be captured in our data, potentially understating participation
from regions where these flagship conferences are less common.


## Affiliation vs. Origin

Geographic data is based on institutional affiliation at the time of
publication, not the author's country of origin. A researcher from China
working at a German institution is counted as a German-affiliated author.
This overstates participation in countries with large international research
communities, such as the United States, and understates participation in
countries with high rates of researcher emigration.


## Coverage

Although most records were successfully matched between DBLP and OpenAlex,
coverage was not complete. Match rates were 94.1% for ICSA and 95.9% for
ICSE. The remaining records were excluded from the analysis.
